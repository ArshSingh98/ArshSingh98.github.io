Embedded Software Engineering at Honda

Developed Embedded Software utilizing Arduino & FreeRTOS to interpret DS18B20 Temperature Sensor to monitor the temperature inside the newly built Heat Transfer System.

After my previous Project at Honda of designing a Heat Transfer System, the Engineering Team required a technician to monitor the temperature inside the heat transfer system. This was done by developing an embedded application using FreeRTOS, Arduino, DS18B20 Temperature sensor and an ESP32 microcontroller. The communication was done through a ADC interface.
