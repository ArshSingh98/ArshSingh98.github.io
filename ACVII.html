Autonomous Capable Vehicle II

Developed Embedded Software in C++ & Assembly to add additional functionality to the Original Line Following Autonomous Vehicle Project.

Mechanical

This was a major upgrade to the original Line Following Autonomous Vehicle Project. Essentially, there was a complete redesign of the vehicle. The plastic chassis were replaced with Aluminum ones which were designed using SolidWorks and cut out using a CNC Machine. This made the chassis future-proof and sturdier than ever.

Electrical

The upgrade here was to develop two more low-side circuits for the other two wheels transforming the cars capabilities from a two-wheel drive to a four-wheel drive. The servomotors were removed as they are flimsy and a new algorithm for turning was developed.
